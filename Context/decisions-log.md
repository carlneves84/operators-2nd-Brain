# Decisions Log

A running log of decisions, with enough context that a future version of you (or a fresh AI session) can understand why a call was made without re-litigating it.

The `weekly-planner` skill appends here automatically. You can also append manually.

---

## Format

```
---

### [Decision Title]
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Source:** [How this came up: meeting name, project, etc.]
- **Context:** [Why this decision was needed -- one or two sentences]
- **Decision:** [What was decided -- one or two sentences]
```
