# Context

This folder is your knowledge base. Everything here is loaded by Aria and the skills when they need background to answer questions or produce output.

## What goes here

Start with these files and add more as your work evolves:

- **learnings.md** -- operational frameworks, learnings from incidents, and patterns you have noticed. Write to this whenever you discover something you do not want to re-discover.
- **decisions-log.md** -- a running log of decisions, with context and reasoning. The `weekly-planner` skill appends here automatically. You can also append manually.
- **annual-recap.md** -- your prior year performance summary. Update once per year.
- **quarterly-recap.md** -- your most recent quarter recap. Update each quarter.
- **vision.md** -- strategic direction, roadmap context, bets in flight.

## Format that works

For each file, short is better than complete. A decision log entry of three sentences beats a three-paragraph writeup that does not get written. A learnings file with ten one-liners is more useful than a comprehensive framework you never finished.

The goal is that a fresh AI session can read these files and understand your context without you re-explaining it. Write to that standard.

## What does not go here

- Week-by-week notes (those go in `Weekly/`)
- Skill outputs and analysis (those go in `Output/`)
- Session handoffs (those go in `Handoffs/`)
- Anything that belongs in your actual documentation platform (Notion, Confluence, etc.) -- context files are AI-readable summaries, not your system of record
